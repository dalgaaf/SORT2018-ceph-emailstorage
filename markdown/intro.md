### Till Luminous
* 6 month release cycle
* every second release were LTS
* too slow LTS cycle
* non-LTS used in production sometimes

### With Mimic
* 9 month release cycle, all releases are stable
* Bugfix backport for two subsequent releases
* Upgrade support for N-2
